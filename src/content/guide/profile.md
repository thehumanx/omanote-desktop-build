# Profile & account

Your profile — name, email, and avatar — is managed through your account provider. omanote doesn't store a separate profile; it uses what your account gives it.

## Where to find your profile

Tap your avatar in the bottom navigation bar (mobile) or the navigation bar (desktop) to open the profile menu. Here you'll see:

- Your **display name** and **email address**
- An **Edit** link that opens your account settings

## Changing your name or avatar

Click **Edit** in the profile menu. This opens `accounts.omanote.com/user` in a new tab — your Clerk-managed account page where you can update your display name, profile photo, and other account details. Changes you make there sync back to omanote automatically.

## What the greeting uses

Your display name feeds into the dynamic greeting you see on the canvas (see **Dynamic greetings**). If you haven't set a name, the greeting falls back to a simple "there."

## Email in your profile

Your email is shown masked in the profile menu (e.g., `a***@example.com`) — it's never displayed in full anywhere in the app. If you need to change your email, use the same Edit link to your account settings.
