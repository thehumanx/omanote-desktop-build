# Public links

Sometimes you want to show someone a set of links, notes, or todos without giving them an account or access to the rest of your workspace. omanote lets you share a **folder** as a public link.

## Sharing a folder

From a bookmark category, note folder, or todo folder, turn on sharing to get a public link. Anyone with the link can view that folder's contents in a clean read-only page — no sign-in required.

## What viewers see

The shared page shows a **snapshot** of the folder's contents, published by you. Viewers see exactly what you shared and nothing else — none of your other folders, and no way to edit. For bookmark and note shares, you can choose whether links appear as **cards** or a compact **list**.

## Privacy of shares

Only the folder you explicitly share is public, and only as the snapshot you pushed. The rest of your workspace stays encrypted and private. You can turn sharing off at any time, which disables the link.

## Views

Shared folders keep a simple view count so you can see whether your link is being opened.
