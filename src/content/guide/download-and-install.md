# Download & install

omanote runs everywhere you do. Pick the version that fits your flow — they all sync through the same encrypted account.

## Web app (recommended for most people)

The quickest way to start — no installation needed. Open [omanote.com](https://omanote.com) in your browser and sign up. It works on any device with a modern browser.

**Install as a PWA** for a near-native experience: in Chrome or Edge, click the install icon in the address bar (or the menu → Install omanote). This adds omanote to your taskbar or home screen with its own window, offline support, and faster loading — no app store needed. On mobile, your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option does the same thing, giving you a full-screen app-like experience until the dedicated mobile apps ship.

## Desktop app (macOS, Windows, Linux)

The desktop app wraps omanote in a native window with its own tray icon, auto-updates, and deeper OS integration. Download the latest installer from the [releases page](https://github.com/thehumanx/omanote-releases/releases/latest):

- **macOS**: `.dmg` file — open it and drag omanote to your Applications folder.
- **Windows**: `.msi` installer — run it and follow the prompts.
- **Linux**: `.deb` or `.AppImage` — pick the package format that matches your distribution.

The desktop app checks for updates in the background every 30 minutes. When a new version is available, a banner appears at the bottom of the screen — click it to download and restart.

## Browser extension (Chrome & Firefox)

The extension adds quick capture to any browser tab without switching windows. Install it from:

- [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/omanote/foafmfgfdbdiiggmmfdoalgpfhkejbjn) (also works in Edge, Brave, Opera, and other Chromium-based browsers)
- [Firefox Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/omanote/)

Once installed, click the toolbar icon or press **Alt+Shift+O** to open quick capture from any page. You can also right-click selected text to save it as a note or bookmark. The extension uses the same encryption as the main app — nothing leaves your device unencrypted.

No account is needed to install the extension. Sign in once to sync with your workspace.

## Mobile app (Android & iOS)

Native mobile apps are in active development. Until they're ready, the web app is fully functional on mobile — bookmarked to your home screen as a PWA, it works like a native app: full-screen, offline-capable, with push notifications on supported browsers.
