# RSS reader

omanote includes an optional RSS reader, so the writing you want to follow lives in the same calm space as everything else. It's off by default — turn it on in **Settings → Features**.

## Subscribing to feeds

Add a feed by its URL, or the address of a site that publishes one. Organize your subscriptions into categories, the same folder idea used across omanote.

## Reading

Feeds show their latest items in a familiar list-and-reader layout: pick a feed or category on the left, read on the right. Items you've read are tracked, and you can mark a whole feed as read to clear the backlog.

## Saving articles

Save an article to come back to it later — saved pieces are kept in their own view, so a good read never gets lost in the stream.
