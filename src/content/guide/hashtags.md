# Hashtags & Explore

Hashtags are the thread that ties your artifacts together across types. Tag a note, a todo, and an event with the same `#tag`, and omanote knows they belong to the same thing.

## Tagging

Type `#` in any note, todo, or event and an autocomplete list of your existing tags appears — pick one or make a new one. Tags render as colored chips so they're easy to scan, and each tag keeps a consistent color wherever it shows up.

## The Explore map

The **Explore** view draws your hashtags as a map — a mindmap-style graph showing which tags connect to which items, and to each other. It's a way to see the shape of what you've been thinking about, and to jump straight to everything under a tag.

## Why it helps

Folders organize by *where* something lives; hashtags organize by *what it's about*. A `#project-x` tag can gather the meeting notes, the follow-up todos, and the events from that project — even though they're different artifact types in different folders.
